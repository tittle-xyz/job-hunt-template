# Cover Letter Template

Copy this to `cover_letters/tailored/<company>.md` (gitignored) and fill it in.
Your contact details live in `profile/profile.yaml` — copy them across, or let your
agent do it.

There's deliberately no generator here. A resume is structured data and benefits
from being built; a cover letter that reads like it was assembled by a machine is
worse than no cover letter at all.

---

**{{YOUR_NAME}}**
{{YOUR_LOCATION}}
{{YOUR_PHONE}} | {{YOUR_EMAIL}}

{{DATE}}

{{HIRING_MANAGER_NAME}}
{{COMPANY_NAME}}
{{COMPANY_ADDRESS}}

---

Dear {{HIRING_MANAGER_NAME, or "Hiring Manager"}},

{{OPENING — why this role, at this company. Be specific about what actually caught
your attention. If the paragraph would survive a find-and-replace of the company
name, it isn't doing any work.}}

{{BODY — one or two paragraphs connecting your experience to what they need. Lead
with outcomes, not responsibilities. Pull concrete detail from your job bullets
rather than restating your resume in prose.}}

{{CLOSING — say what you want to happen next.}}

Best,
{{YOUR_NAME}}

---

## Notes for customization

- Avoid: "I am writing to express my interest" (generic opener)
- Avoid: em-dashes, "leverage", "synergy", "passionate about"
- Do: reference specific things about the company and the role
- Do: use concrete examples from your experience
- Do: keep it under one page
- Do: match tone to company culture (startup vs enterprise)
